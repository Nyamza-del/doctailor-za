The Problem

Why good candidates
get ignored

It is not always about your experience. It is about how your documents communicate that experience to the right people.

  • 📋
    You send the same CV to every job. Employers and ATS systems scan for specific words from their own advert. If those words are not in your CV, you are filtered out before a human ever reads it.
  • ✉️
    Your cover letter is generic. "I am writing to apply for…" is how most letters start. Hiring managers have seen it a thousand times. Yours gets skipped.
  • 📧
    Your application email says nothing. Most people paste "Please find attached my CV." The email itself is a first impression — and a missed opportunity.
  • 🔍
    You do not know what to change. You know something is wrong but you do not know what. That uncertainty is exhausting and it stalls applications.

Here is what we do about it.

We take your CV and the job you are applying for, and we rewrite your documents to speak the exact language of that role. Every keyword. Every requirement. Every tone. Your experience stays true — we just present it in a way that gets you past the filter and onto the shortlist. It takes us hours. For you, it can change everything.

Our Services

Three documents.
One application package.

Not sure what each one is? We explain it plainly below. You choose what you need.

01
CV Tailoring

Your CV is rewritten to match the specific job you are applying for. We use the same language as the job advert, highlight your most relevant experience, and structure it so it passes automated screening systems (ATS) and impresses human reviewers.

What is ATS? — A computer system many companies use to filter CVs before a person reads them. If your CV does not match their keywords, it never reaches a human. We fix that.
02
Cover Letter

A cover letter is a short professional letter that introduces you before the employer reads your CV. Many roles require one. Even when they do not, a strong cover letter can be the reason you get the call. We write it in your voice, specifically for the role — no generic templates.

Do I need one? — Always include one if you can. It gives you space to explain things a CV cannot. It shows you took the application seriously.
03
Application Email

The email you send with your application is the first thing the recruiter reads — before they open a single attachment. Most people write two lines and wonder why no one responds. We write a short, purposeful email that sets the right tone and makes them want to open your documents.

Why does this matter? — Because it is the first impression. A strong email signals professionalism before a single document is opened.

Good to Know

Questions we get asked
all the time.

What is ATS and why does it matter?
ATS stands for Applicant Tracking System. Many medium and large companies use it to automatically scan CVs before a human ever reads them. It filters out any CV that does not contain the right keywords from the job advert. If your CV is not tailored, it often gets rejected automatically — not because you are not qualified, but because the system did not see the right words.
Do you change my actual experience or qualifications?
Never. We do not invent experience or exaggerate qualifications. What we do is reframe and reword what you already have, so it is presented in the language the employer used in their job advert. Your facts stay yours. We just make sure they land.
What format will I receive my documents in?
You will receive your final documents as a PDF file, sent via WhatsApp or email. PDFs are the safest format for applications because they preserve the formatting regardless of what device or software the employer uses to open them.
When do I pay?
You pay after you have seen the preview and you are happy with the work. We send you a screenshot via WhatsApp first. Once you approve, you pay via eWallet and we deliver the final PDF. No upfront payment required for your first order.
Is my personal information safe?
Yes. Your documents are used only to complete your tailoring request and are not stored, shared or sold. We operate in line with the Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA). Your data is handled with full confidentiality.
What if I am not happy with the result?
You preview before you pay. If something does not feel right, tell us via WhatsApp and we will revise it. We want you to walk away confident about your application — not uncertain. Your satisfaction is what builds our reputation.
